FIND TUTORS

We understand that good tutoring is difficult to find, especially if your wallet’s been stretched thin! You scour khanAcademy and Youtube for
educational videos to teach yourself or your children, but those videos are inevitably one size fits all, and only leaves the future inheritors of this
world more confused than ever. To combat this pervasive issue, we compiled an extensive list of free tutoring services for the struggling student to use!

OSAT(One Step At A Time)

With over 50 tutors in its ranks, this organization is a rising power, chock full of dedicated and passionate high schoolers to lead the younger
generations. OSAT covers Math, Science, English, World Languages, and Computer Science, and teaches any student from Kindergarten to
eighth grade. For the older students who still want to further their learning, SAT Tutoring is also availiable! To receive tutoring, simply fill
out a form in the website, under the “Forms” section!
For more information, visit their website
